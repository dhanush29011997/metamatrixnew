BIM Implementation

For renovation or retrofit projects, we convert laser scan data (point clouds) into precise BIM models. This service is essential for capturing the as-built conditions of existing structures for accurate modeling and planning.

3D Modeling

Our 3D BIM modeling service transforms traditional blueprints and plans into dynamic, three-dimensional models. This allows for an immersive visualization experience, enabling stakeholders to explore the design in a virtual environment

4D Scheduling

Integrating time elements into our BIM models, our 4D scheduling service enhances project timeline visualization. This facilitates better planning and tracking of construction phases, ensuring projects stay on schedule.
